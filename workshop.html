Urgency Print Lab
A slow web workshop in ten steps.
120 min








1—Take out your device. Set it to not go to sleep.






2—Choose one image from your camera roll to share with the group.
5 min






3—Let’s gather the devices together to take a look.
10 min






4—Now, put your device away.
5—Take some time to think about the images and messages that you’ve seen recently on the web.



What stands out?
What’s missing?
Who/what do you wish you hadn’t seen?
Who/what should be more visible?
What’s got value?
What’s worth circulating?



Write about the images and messages that you wish for. Be as specific as possible.
10 min







6—Now, open your device to access the web.



7—Look for images and messages that should be slowed down and examined more closely. Search for urgent material. It could be a single tweet or image, or someone’s entire feed. Make some selections and arrange them on your desktop. Feel free to incorporate your writing, too.
20 min



8— Now let’s scan and print!
20 min



9— Cut, tape, draw, write, and collage the printed material onto 8.5 x 11 sheets. These will become the pages of our collaborative publication.
40 min

10—Circulate the URL! artbook.printedweb.org to download our publication: Urgencyprintlab.pdf
